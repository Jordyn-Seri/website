## Personal Brand Website for Jordyn Seri, PMP

### Design System

- **Palette**: olive (#6F7C12), Tropical Teal (#17BEBB), Lilac Ash (#ADA8B6),  Deep Space Blue (#172A3A), and Lime Cream (#DAFF7D), with subtle gray accents
- **Typography**: Inter or similar modern sans-serif, large headlines, generous white space
- **Vibe**: Enterprise professional meets tech startup — clean, high-trust, conversion-focused

### Pages & Sections (Single-Page Layout)

1. **Sticky Navigation** — Logo/name, section links (Who I Help, Impact, Credentials, About), "Schedule a Consult" CTA button
2. **Hero Section** — Bold headline, sub-headline, teal CTA button linking to Calendly/contact. Subtle gradient or geometric background pattern in line cream/teal.
3. **"Who I Help" Section** — Three cards with icons for Health Systems, Clinics, and Startups, each with the provided descriptions. Clean grid layout.
4. **Case Studies / Impact Section** — Three Problem → Solution → Result cards with bold metric highlights (3,500+ claims, 14,000+ members, 20+ partners). Teal accent numbers.
5. **Testimonials Slider** — Carousel with placeholder quotes from Boutique Consulting Firm CO, Medicaid Program Director, and Startup Founder. Clean card design with role/title.
6. **Credentials & Tools** — Grid of certification badges/pills (PMP,  Monday.com) and skills list (Dashboard Building, Healthcare Compliance, Continuous Improvement). Include the following embedded YouTube video: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wVxn60tBu4c](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wVxn60tBu4c)
7. **"The Human Behind the Data"** — Personal section with outdoor interests (rock climbing, mountain biking), warm tone, placeholder for personal photo.
8. **Footer** — LinkedIn link, email, "Currently accepting new clients" badge, back-to-top button.

### Interactions

- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Testimonial carousel with auto-play and manual controls
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-in animations
- Mobile-responsive throughout
